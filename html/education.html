I graduated from HUN SEN Saang High School in 2021. During my time there, I was truly happy. The environment was welcoming and supportive, even though the school was smaller compared to a university. I cherished the ample time I had to spend with my friends, creating lasting memories. The teachers were exceptional, providing us with not only strong academic training but also valuable advice and life lessons. Their dedication contributed greatly to our personal and intellectual growth, making our high school years some of the best in our lives.

In the past few months, I graduated from the PUC-Institute of Foreign Languages, and it was an incredibly happy time for me. The teachers were not just educators but also kind mentors who guided me with patience. My friends were friendly and supportive, making every moment enjoyable. I really miss them all. The connections I made there and the experiences we shared left a lasting impact on me. Moving on is bittersweet, but I carry with me fond memories and the hope that these friendships will endure.

My time there was filled with both fun and challenging moments, creating memories that I will never forget. I deeply miss my friends from CSTAD, and I am truly grateful to the president and my teachers for their support and guidance.
